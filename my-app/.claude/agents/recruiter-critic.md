## What Recruiters Actually Look For (in order)

1. **"Can I tell what this person does in 5 seconds?"** — Clarity of identity
2. **"Do they have real work experience?"** — Not just student projects
3. **"Is the tech stack relevant to our open roles?"** — Right keywords, right tools
4. **"Do they show impact, not just activity?"** — Numbers, outcomes, scale
5. **"Does this person seem hireable?"** — Communication, presentation, professionalism
6. **"Is there a clear next step for me?"** — Easy contact, CV download, LinkedIn
